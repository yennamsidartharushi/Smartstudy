Important Questions

Repeated questions from the last 10 years of SSC TS Board Exams.

Mathematics

1. Real Numbers

  • Prove that √3 is irrational.
  • Prove that √5 is irrational.
  • Show that 3 + 2√5 is irrational.
  • Find HCF and LCM of 306 and 657 using Euclid’s algorithm.
  • Use Euclid’s Division Algorithm to find the HCF of 135 and 225.

2. Polynomials

  • Find the zeroes of the quadratic polynomial x² - 7x + 10.
  • Find the zeroes of the quadratic polynomial x² - 5x + 6.
  • Verify the relationship between zeroes and coefficients.

3. Pair of Linear Equations

  • Solve the system: 2x + 3y = 13 and 3x - y = 5.

4. Quadratic Equations

  • Solve using quadratic formula: x² - 7x + 10 = 0.
  • Solve using quadratic formula: x² - 5x + 6 = 0.

5. Arithmetic Progressions

  • Find the sum of first 20 terms of an AP: 3, 7, 11, 15...
  • Find the 20th term of AP: 3, 7, 11, 15...
  • Find the sum of first 30 terms of AP.

6. Trigonometry

  • Prove the trigonometric identity: 1/sinθ - 1/cscθ = 0.
  • Prove that (1 - tan²θ) / (1 + tan²θ) = cos 2θ.

7. Coordinate Geometry & Statistics

  • Find area of triangle formed by points (5,2), (4,7), (7,-4).
  • Find mean, median, and mode of grouped data.

8. Mensuration

  • Find the surface area and volume of a cone.
Science

Physics

  • State Ohm’s Law and explain with a graph.
  • Difference between AC and DC current.
  • Explain the Electric Motor with a labelled diagram.
  • What is the refraction of light?
  • Define focal length and power of a lens. Find focal length when power is given.

Chemistry

  • Write types of chemical reactions with examples.
  • Explain corrosion and rancidity.
  • Write the chemical properties of acids and bases.
  • Explain the electrolysis of water with a diagram.

Biology

  • Explain the Human Digestive System with a diagram.
  • Explain Respiration in human beings.
  • Describe Blood circulation in the human heart.
  • Explain Reproduction in plants.
  • Explain the Human reproductive system.
Social Studies

History

  • Causes and results of World War I.
  • Consequences & effects of World War II.
  • Importance of the Bandung Conference.

Civics

  • What is Democracy? Explain its features.
  • Functions of the Election Commission.

Geography

  • Explain major rivers of India.
  • Explain types of soils in India.
  • Explain Water resources in India.

Economics

  • What is Globalisation? Explain.
  • Role of Banks in economic development.
English & Hindi

English

  • Write a summary of "The Dear Departed".
  • Character sketch of Mrs. Slater.
  • Write a Notice for a school event.
  • Write an Invitation letter for a school function.
  • Change Active voice to Passive voice.
  • Write an Essay on Environmental Pollution.
  • Write a letter to the headmaster requesting leave.

Hindi

  • Write सारांश (Summary) of a lesson.
  • पत्र लेखन (Letter writing).
  • निबंध लेखन (Essay writing) on पर्यावरण संरक्षण (Environment).
  • अपठित गद्यांश (Unseen passage).
  • कविता का भावार्थ (Poem explanation).
